About the Artist

Elena Vance is a contemporary fine art painter whose work explores the intersection of memory, emotion, and the natural world. With over 15 years of professional experience, her paintings have been exhibited in galleries across Europe and North America.

Her distinctive style combines classical techniques with modern sensibilities, creating works that are both timeless and contemporary. Each piece is a journey into color, texture, and form, inviting viewers to explore their own emotional landscapes.

Biography

Elena Vance was born in Florence, Italy, where she developed an early appreciation for Renaissance art and classical techniques. She studied at the Accademia di Belle Arti di Firenze before moving to New York to complete her MFA at the School of Visual Arts.

Her work has been described as "a delicate balance between chaos and order, where emotion finds form through color and texture." Elena's paintings are held in private collections worldwide and have been featured in numerous solo and group exhibitions.

When not in her studio, Elena teaches masterclasses in contemporary painting techniques and serves on the board of several arts organizations dedicated to supporting emerging artists.

The Commission Process

Working together to create your perfect piece

Commissioning a piece of art is a collaborative process that results in a unique work created specifically for you and your space. Elena works closely with clients to understand their vision, preferences, and the environment where the artwork will be displayed.

The process typically involves an initial consultation, concept development, creation of the artwork, and final delivery. Commissioned pieces range in size, medium, and complexity, with pricing determined by these factors.
